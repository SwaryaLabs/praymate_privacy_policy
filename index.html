Privacy Policy for Praymate

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Welcome to Praymate ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application "Praymate" (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us and information collected automatically when you use the App.

1.1. Personal Information

When you sign up for an account, we collect the following information:

  • Google Sign-In: If you choose to sign up using your Google account, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture URL provided by Google.
  • Email/Password: If you choose to sign up using email, we collect your email address and a hashed version of your password.

1.2. Location Information

To provide the core functionality of finding nearby mosques, the App requests access to your device's location services.

  • Precise Location: We use your precise location (GPS and network-based) to query the Google Places Nearby Search API. This allows us to display mosques in your immediate vicinity.
  • Usage: This location data is processed in real-time to fetch mosque details. We do not store your historical location trail on our servers unless required for specific community features you opt into.

1.3. User-Generated Content (Community Data)

Praymate relies on a community of users to maintain accurate Jamaat (congregation) schedules.

  • If you choose to add or update a Jamaat schedule for a mosque, the information you submit (prayer times, notes) will be visible to other users of the App.
  • This data may be associated with your User ID to ensure accountability and prevent spam.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Identify nearby mosques using Google Places API.
  • Send you push notifications for prayer times based on your selected preferences (5, 10, or 15 minutes prior).
  • Manage the "Do Not Disturb" mode automation on your device during prayer times.
  • Maintain the database of mosque schedules through community updates.
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests.

3. Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions on your Android device to function correctly:

  • Location (Fine/Coarse): Required to find mosques near you.
  • Notification: Required to send prayer time reminders.
  • Do Not Disturb Access: Required to automatically silence your phone during prayer times and restore it afterward, as per your settings.

4. Third-Party Services

The App utilizes third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

  • Google Sign-In Services: For authentication.
  • Google Places API / Google Maps: For locating mosques and retrieving place details.
  • NodeJS Server: For database management and authentication storage.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

5. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable, and no method of data transmission can be guaranteed against any interception or other type of misuse.

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you are under the age of 13, please do not submit any personal information through our App. We encourage parents and legal guardians to monitor their children's Internet usage and to help enforce this Policy by instructing their children never to provide personal information through our App without their permission.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
